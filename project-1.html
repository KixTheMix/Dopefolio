The Shoe (2026) PC

A passion project bite-size hybrid visual novel about losing your shoe at a party.

Made by 2 people over the summer of 2025 + January 2026 using Unity, Adobe Photoshop & Yarnspinner.

Download Game

Project Description

The Shoe puts the player in quite the situation. You're drunk at a houseparty in the middle of the winter and as you leave you realize one small detail... Someone has stolen your other Matsune Hiku shoe. But as you search the house and to confront the thief you realize that your perception of the world has been twisted.

This game was a passion project by me and my significant other, I was the Writer/Narrative Designer while he was the Game Designer/Artist/Programmer. We wanted to make a fun and short game, and the idea stayed an idea until the summer of 2025 when it began development. We finished the game in January 2026 after putting it on the back-burner since September 2025.

Writing

The challenge I set for myself was to make something anxiety inducing into something comedic.

The Goals
- I wanted the story to be immersive to the player where the added visual element would only enhance it.
- There needed to be sillyness sprinkled throughout the story as it wasn't supposed to be serious.
- The feeling of being drunk was essential to the story and would tie in with the comedy. It would also further the immersion for the player. It would also act as a struggle for the player as the character would be unable to see faces.
- A hook was needed to interest and engage the player in the start, but the player also had to be motivated to follow the character throughout the whole game.
